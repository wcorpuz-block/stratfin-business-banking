# Block Business Banking Competitive Analysis Framework

## Research Areas

### 1. Instant Transfer Competitors
- **Traditional Banks**: Wells Fargo, Chase, Bank of America
  - Fee structures: $15-25 for same-day transfers
  - Processing times: 1-3 business days standard, same-day premium
  
- **Fintech Competitors**: Stripe, PayPal, Wise
  - Fee models: 0.5-1.5% + fixed fees
  - Speed: Real-time to 1 business day
  
- **Key Differentiators to Research**:
  - API integration capabilities
  - Multi-currency support
  - Regulatory compliance (ACH, wire transfer rules)
  - Customer experience and onboarding

### 2. Business Credit Card Market
- **Traditional Players**: American Express, Chase Ink, Capital One Spark
  - Interchange rates: 1.5-2.5%
  - Annual fees: $0-$695
  - Rewards programs: 1-5% cashback/points
  
- **Fintech Disruptors**: Brex, Ramp, Divvy
  - Focus on expense management integration
  - Real-time spend controls
  - Automated accounting integrations
  
- **Market Size**: ~$200B annual spend on business credit cards

### 3. Business Debit Card Landscape
- **Bank Solutions**: Most major banks offer basic business debit
  - Limited features, basic online banking
  - Interchange: ~0.5-0.8%
  
- **Modern Solutions**: Mercury, Novo, Azlo (discontinued)
  - Enhanced digital experience
  - Spend categorization and analytics
  - Integration with accounting software
