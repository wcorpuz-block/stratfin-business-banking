## Key Financial Insights

### 1. Credit Card Drives Portfolio Value
- Highest revenue per customer ($210.70/month)
- Largest absolute revenue contribution
- Strong unit economics with 72.5% profit margin

### 2. Instant Transfer Shows Strong Margins
- Excellent profit margins (72.3%)
- Significant transaction volume potential
- High take rate creates sustainable revenue

### 3. Debit Card Provides Steady Base Revenue
- Lower revenue per customer but highest profit margin (80.8%)
- Predictable monthly fee revenue
- Low operational complexity

### 4. Scalability Analysis
- Linear scaling across all products
- No significant economies of scale in current model
- Opportunity to optimize unit economics at higher volumes

## Risk Factors and Sensitivities

2. **Interchange Rates:** Regulatory changes could affect debit/credit margins
3. **Customer Acquisition Costs:** Higher CAC could extend payback periods
4. **Transaction Volumes:** Economic downturns could reduce usage
