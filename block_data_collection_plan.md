# Block Business Banking Data Collection Plan

## Key Stakeholders to Connect With

### Finance Team
- **CFO Office**: Overall business banking P&L and strategic metrics
- **FP&A Team**: Historical forecasts and variance analysis
- **Accounting**: Revenue recognition policies and cost allocation methods
- **Treasury**: Funding costs and capital allocation for banking products

### Product Teams
- **Business Banking Product Manager**: Product roadmap and feature priorities
- **Payments Product Team**: Instant transfer technical specifications and costs
- **Card Product Team**: Credit and debit card program details

### Operations & Risk
- **Risk Management**: Credit policies, fraud rates, and loss provisions
- **Compliance**: Regulatory requirements and associated costs
- **Operations**: Processing volumes, customer service costs, operational metrics

### Data & Analytics
- **Data Science Team**: Customer behavior analysis and predictive models
- **Business Intelligence**: Existing dashboards and reporting infrastructure
- **Customer Analytics**: Segmentation and lifetime value models

## Critical Data Points to Collect

### Historical Performance Data (Last 24 months)
- [ ] Transaction volumes by product (monthly/quarterly)
- [ ] Revenue by product line and revenue stream
- [ ] Customer acquisition numbers and costs
- [ ] Customer churn rates and retention metrics
- [ ] Average transaction values and frequency
- [ ] Processing costs and operational expenses
- [ ] Fraud losses and charge-off rates

### Current Product Specifications
- [ ] Fee structures and pricing models currently in use
- [ ] Credit policies and underwriting criteria
- [ ] Processing partners and associated costs
- [ ] Regulatory capital requirements
- [ ] Technology infrastructure costs

### Market Intelligence
- [ ] Customer feedback and satisfaction scores
- [ ] Competitive win/loss analysis
- [ ] Market share estimates
- [ ] Pipeline and sales funnel metrics

### Forward-Looking Projections
- [ ] Existing forecasts and assumptions
- [ ] Product roadmap and enhancement plans
- [ ] Market expansion opportunities
- [ ] Regulatory changes on the horizon

## Data Collection Methods

### Internal Meetings
1. **Finance Leadership Briefing** (Week 1)
   - Overall business banking strategy and performance
   - Key challenges and opportunities
   - Budget and resource allocation

2. **Product Deep Dives** (Week 1-2)
   - Individual sessions for each product (Instant Transfer, Credit Card, Debit Card)
   - Technical specifications and cost drivers
   - Customer usage patterns and feedback

3. **Operations Review** (Week 2)
   - Processing infrastructure and costs
   - Risk management and compliance requirements
   - Customer service and support metrics

### Data Requests
- [ ] Historical financial statements for business banking segment
- [ ] Customer transaction data (aggregated/anonymized)
- [ ] Competitive analysis reports (if available)
- [ ] Existing financial models and forecasts
- [ ] Regulatory filings and compliance reports

### System Access Requests
- [ ] Business intelligence dashboards
- [ ] Financial reporting systems
- [ ] Customer analytics platforms
- [ ] Risk management systems

## Questions to Prepare for Stakeholder Meetings

### Strategic Questions
1. What are Block's 3-year goals for business banking?
2. How does business banking fit into Block's overall ecosystem strategy?
3. What are the biggest competitive threats and opportunities?
4. Where are we winning vs. losing in the market?

### Financial Questions
1. What are the current unit economics for each product?
2. How do we allocate shared costs across products?
3. What are our customer acquisition cost targets?
4. How do we measure and report profitability?

### Operational Questions
1. What are our key operational constraints or bottlenecks?
2. How scalable is our current infrastructure?
3. What regulatory changes are we preparing for?
4. Where are our highest risk areas?
